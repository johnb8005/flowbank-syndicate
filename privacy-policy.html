FlowBank Recovery Syndicate Privacy Policy

Category Details
Information Collected - Full Name
- Personal Email Address
- Flowbank Account Number
Purpose of Collection - To unite affected customers and depositors for collective action
- To send group communications to the liquidator, FINMA, and relevant institutions
Use of Information - To add participants to the newsletter
- To coordinate and send group emails and communications
- To provide updates and developments
Information Sharing - Information will be shared with the liquidator, FINMA, and relevant institutions as part of the collective action
Data Protection - Personal information will be stored securely
- Access to personal information will be restricted to authorized personnel only
Communication - Participants will receive updates and communications via email
Consent - By providing personal information, participants consent to its use for the purposes outlined in this policy
Opt-Out - Participants can opt-out of communications at any time by emailing flowbankrecoverysyndicate@gmail.com
Contact Information - For any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at flowbankrecoverysyndicate@gmail.com
