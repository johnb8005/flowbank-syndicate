Recovery Syndicate

Welcome to the FlowBank Recovery Syndicate

We are a community of depositors and stakeholders united by a common goal: to recover the maximum amount of funds possible from the FlowBank bankruptcy. Together, we can pool our resources, share critical information, and support each other through this challenging process.

About Us

Our Mission

  • Gather Information: Collect and share relevant information regarding the bankruptcy proceedings.
  • Unite Efforts: Bring together depositors and stakeholders to amplify our collective voice.
  • Maximize Recovery: Work towards retrieving the highest possible amount of funds for all members.

Projects/Updates

Current Initiatives

  • Information Gathering: We are actively collecting documents, news, and legal updates related to FlowBank's bankruptcy.
  • Legal Support: Coordinating with legal experts to explore all possible avenues for fund recovery.
  • Member Advocacy: Representing the interests of our members in discussions with liquidators and financial authorities.

News and Updates

  • June 12, 2024: Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority (FINMA) initiated bankruptcy proceedings against FlowBank SA.
  • June 13, 2024, 08:00 AM: Bankruptcy proceedings took effect.
  • June 19, 2024: Establishment of the Recovery Syndicate.
